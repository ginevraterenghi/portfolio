Weeping Bergs
Can Beauty survive the Climate Change?
Info-poetry
2018
The project has been developed for Final Synthesis Studio during the second year of the Master class, where the idea of info-poetry begin. An info-poetry is a data representation using artistic techniques, which try to communicate some specific information engaging the feelings of the public.
My info-poetry is about the iceberg melting and try to offer a different prospective on the climate change issue which has been reported, in addition to the known effects, of destroying the Beauty. The project is driven by the question: Can Beauty survive the Climate Change? Icergs perform the kind of Beauty which has been in dengerous by the Climate Change effects. These ice blocks are melting faster and faster, changing the landscape they create.
The dataset I refearred to realize the project considers the icebergs formed starting from the 1978 in the Southern Emisphere. Data comes from the International official sources of the entity take care of the Iceberg monitoring (the main: Internationa Ice Patrol, North America Ice Service, National Snow and Ice Data Center, National Ocean Service).
Every Iceberg is represented by a paper. Where white, the Iceberg is not completely meelted and so, already alive, while the blue spots standing for the Iceberg which are extinguished. To realize the spots I created small blue ice cubes which I leave to meelt on the paper. Because of the methods adopted every spot has a different and unique shape. The order observe the born date of the Icebergs.
Than, I composed a sound track considering day, month and year of born and death of each iceberg. The sound emerging from the dataset experiments a different way to highlight the icberg life rythm.
